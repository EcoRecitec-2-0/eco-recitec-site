    INTERNATIONAL CONGRESS ON CIRCULAR ECONOMY, SKILLS, AND TRANSFORMATIVE TECHNOLOGIES




    It is an event that will bring together experts, researchers, and professionals from the fields of Sustainability, Psychology, Humanities, Technology, Startups, and Investors.
    The aim is to discuss the latest trends, share knowledge, promote networking, and conduct circular business in technological ecosystems.
    The event integrates the areas of Humanities, Psychology, Sustainability, Environment, and Technologies, with a focus on Artificial Intelligence, in support of a Humanitarian and Sustainable Purpose.
